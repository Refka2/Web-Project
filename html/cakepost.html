Homemade Vanilla Cake Success Tips

Learn from my mistakes and bake the best cake on the 1st try!

username
  1. Follow the recipe closely.
  2. Use each power ingredient listed.
  3. Use room temperature ingredients.
  4. The batter mixes together evenly when all the cake ingredients are roughly the same temperature.
    5. This also reduces the risk of over-mixing and over-baking.
  5. Set out your ingredients 1 hour before beginning.
  6. Line your cake pans with parchment. Place your cake pans on a large sheet of parchment paper.
  7. Grease the pan and the parchment paper.
  8. Parchment paper rounds guarantee seamless removal from the pan because the cake slides right out.
  9. Cool cake layers completely.
  10. Make sure each layer is cool– refrigerate or freeze the layers if you need to!
  11. Refrigerate decorated cake. After frosting the cake, place it in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour.
  12. You’ll get beautifully clean slices because the crumbs are cool and tight.
